Connect 4 - PopOut

Played: 500

Game Instructions

1. The game is played on a vertical board with seven columns and six rows.

2. Players take turns to drop colored coins from the top into a column.

3. The coin falls straight down, occupying the next available space within the column.

4. The objective is to be the first to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of four of one's own coins.

5. The game ends in a draw if the board is completely filled without any player forming a line of four coins.
